Common Questions

Frequently asked questions

Is root canal treatment painful?

Modern root canal treatment is performed under local anaesthesia and is generally painless. Most patients feel only mild discomfort for a day or two afterward.

How long do dental implants last?

Dental implants can last 15–25 years or longer with good oral hygiene and regular dental checkups, making them one of the most durable tooth replacement options.

When should I visit a dentist?

Visit every six months for a routine checkup, or sooner if you notice tooth pain, bleeding gums, swelling or sensitivity that doesn't settle within a day.

What is the cost of dental implants?

Cost depends on the implant type, number of teeth and your treatment plan. Book a consultation at Shine for a personalised, transparent estimate.

What is the best treatment for missing teeth?

Dental implants are generally the best long-term option for missing teeth, offering a natural look and bite strength close to a real tooth. Bridges and dentures remain suitable alternatives depending on your case.

Is financing available?

Yes — flexible payment plans and insurance partnerships are available. Ask our front desk for current options.

What about dental emergencies?

We offer priority access for existing patients facing sudden pain, trauma or a broken restoration. Call the clinic directly for the fastest response.
